Our Story

Building the backbone of
African manufacturing

Forge Africa exists to make manufacturing accessible, transparent, and efficient for every business in Nigeria and beyond.

Our Mission

Closing the gap between ideas and production

Every day, businesses across Nigeria struggle to find reliable manufacturers. Phone calls go unanswered. Quotes are inconsistent. Quality is unpredictable. Payments are unprotected.

Forge Africa was built to solve this. We created a structured marketplace where businesses submit detailed manufacturing requests, and our verified vendor network competes to deliver the best value.

Every transaction is managed end-to-end — from RFQ to payment to delivery — with full transparency for both parties.
