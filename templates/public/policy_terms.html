4. The RFQ Process

The Forge Africa RFQ process works as follows:

  1. A Client submits a manufacturing request detailing specifications, quantity, and deadline
  2. Forge Africa reviews and approves the RFQ before opening it to vendors
  3. Verified Vendors submit competitive bids within the bidding window
  4. Forge Africa curates the most suitable bid and presents a final quote to the Client
  5. The Client accepts the quote and pays a deposit to initiate the project
  6. The Vendor delivers the order; balance payment is made on satisfactory delivery

Forge Africa acts as an intermediary and facilitator. We are not a party to the manufacturing contract between Client and Vendor.
